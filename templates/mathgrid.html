MATH GRID QUEST
Find adjacent cells that sum to the target
  1. 1
    🎯 Memorize the Target Sum shown at the top of the board.
  2. 2
    🖱️ Click cells to select them — selected cells must be adjacent (up/down/left/right).
  3. 3
    ✅ When your selected cells sum equals the target, press SUBMIT.
  4. 4
    ⭐ A correct match scores 10 pts × number of cells selected.
  5. 5
    ⏱️ The grid refreshes and a new target appears. Keep going until time runs out!
💡 Pro tip: Longer chains score more points. Look for 3-4 cell paths that hit the target!
